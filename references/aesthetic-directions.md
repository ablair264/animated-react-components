# Aesthetic Directions

Use these as prompts to pick a single, bold direction.

- Precision tech: crisp grids, cool neutrals, micro-contrast, thin lines
- Warm editorial: serif display, generous leading, paper textures, muted palette
- Crafted analog: tactile materials, grain, imperfect edges, warm light
- Bold modular: oversized type, strong blocks, primary colors, hard edges
- Neo-natural: organic shapes, earthy palette, soft gradients, gentle motion
- Kinetic futurist: high contrast, neon accents, dynamic motion, depth layers
